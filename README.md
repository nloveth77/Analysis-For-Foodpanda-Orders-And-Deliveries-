# Analysis-For-Foodpanda-Orders-And-Deliveries-

Project Objective;
This project is for Foodpanda Sales and Deliveries and it’s focused on analyzing their quantity of sales and deliveries across their different restaurants in varying cities. This analysis further aims at making potential customers see the varying Dish names and Food categories they sell, and their methods of delivery and efficiency in the business.

Introduction;
The major dataset used for this analysis is the Sales and Delivery Report for Foodpanda. The key methodology used is Microsoft Excel , and the Pivot table in Microsoft Excel was the major tool used to address and answer all of the project questions.

Story of Data:

The dataset is a sales and Delivery report dataset available for further analysis. The dataset was downloaded from kaggle.com and it has a total number of 6001 rows and 19 columns.

The dataset is structured in columns and rows. The columns contain variables, both Dependent and Independent Variables. And the rows contain the corresponding data points, and figures.

The analysis includes Quantity, Price, Churned and Rating as the key outcome variable and other variables such as Dish Name, Category, Churned, Gender, Age, City, and Restaurant Name. These variables basically contribute to this analysis as they are key factors to addressing and answering questions from the project.

There were no limitations during the analysis as the data is already clean.

Data Splitting and Preprocessing:

I got the dataset downloaded from kaggle.com and uploaded it into my Excel Workbook for further analysis. The major Stakeholders of this project are the Location Managers and Sales Managers.

To this business, success means generating more revenues for the business.

-ANALYSIS- Most Preferred Dish Name

Observations:

1. The dish name pasta recorded the highest rating from customers, making it the most preferred dish name.

2. Pizza and Fries also received good ratings from customers.

3. Burger dish has the least rating from customers amongst other dishes, which makes it the least preferred.

-ANALYSIS- Top Restaurant Name

Observations:

1. Subway restaurants have the highest rating from customers, which tops it as number 1 amongst others.

2. KFC restaurants and Pizza Hut restaurants have almost the same number of ratings from their customers.

3. Burger King and McDonald’s restaurants have the lowest ratings from customers.

-ANALYSIS: Best Category

Observations:

1. The Italian food category turns out as the best category amongst other categories as larger quantities of it were sold out .

2. Continental , Fast food and Chinese categories were also sold out in large quantities.

3. The dessert category has the least quantity of orders and deliveries.

-ANALYSIS- Best City By Revenue

Observations:

1. Multan city generated the highest revenue of $1,009,003.67 from the sales of their dishes and categories in all Foodpanda restaurants within the city.

2. Peshawar city and Lahore city also generated a good volume of revenue from the sales of all their dishes and categories.

3. Karachi city generated the lowest revenue of $900,176.82 from their sales.

-ANALYSIS- Age Churned

Observations:

1. From this analysis, the age group adults are more inactive by 963 counts with their food orders than the Teenager and Senior age group.

2. The Senior age is also inactive by 1005 counts.

3. Because teenagers like eating out, they are very much active and less inactive.

In-Analysis Insights:

-ANALYSIS- Gender Churned;

1. Seeing that the female gender patronizes the business more, discounts can be given to the female customers as a way of appreciating and encouraging their patronage.

2. More similar varieties of the kind of food the male gender buys should be made available to boost their orders.

3. An observation should be made as to why the other gender is inactive in their orders and purchases.

-ANALYSIS- Most Preferred Dish Name;

1. The quality of pasta and sandwich dishes should be maintained as it meets customer satisfaction.

2. The quality and recipe used for pizza and fries should be enhanced and improved to motivate and encourage more sales from these dish names.

3. A questionnaire can be handed to customers to see views and comments on burger dishes, so that useful measures would be taken to improve the sales of burger dishes.

-ANALYSIS- Top Restaurant Name;

1. Subway restaurants should keep up with their services, recipes, menus and operations as it makes their customers want to patronize them the most.

2. KFC restaurant and Pizza Hut restaurant have great potentials of becoming the top restaurants in the coming year by ensuring they upgrade their operations and services.

3. McDonald’s restaurants and Burger King restaurants have to do quick and intentional checks on their operations, services, recipes and menus and ensure major changes are done.

-ANALYSIS — Best Category;

1. Seeing that Italian Category has a large number of orders for it, the chefs that make them should often upgrade their recipes for it to make sure it keeps selling out in larger quantities.

2. Continental, Fast food and Chinese Categories should be recommended more to customers for further promotion and awareness in all the restaurants.

3. Because customers don’t really order dessert in large quantities, it shouldn’t be prepared much in the restaurants.

-ANALYSIS- Best City by Revenue;

1. The Multan city should keep up with the price of their dishes and categories as it is worth the food quality to ensure continuous inflow of great revenues to the business.

2. Pershawar City and Lahore City should do more advertising their foods to ensure more customers patronize the Foodpanda restaurants.

3. Karachi City has to put in more work in all the Foodpanda restaurants within the city to boost revenue.

-ANALYSIS- Age Churned Analysis;

1. Knowing that the adult age isn’t much active with placing orders, the cooks or chefs and service personnel's in the Foodpanda restaurants should better understand the kind of recipes the adult age preferred for their food, to improve their active counts and patronage.

2. The same methodology used for the age group can also be replicated for the senior age, to ensure their active orders.

Restaurants like Subway, KFC, McDonalds, and Pizza hut sold out more of this food category than the other categories and cities like Lahore, Multan and Islamabad generated good revenue from the sales of this category.

3. Multan city generated the highest revenue of $1,009,003.67 amongst other cities. The top restaurants in this city are Subway, pizza hut, KFC and McDonalds restaurants. The other gender customers are more active than the male and female gender in this city and they are more of the senior age. They preferred dishes like fries, sandwich, burger, pasta and pizza and the best food categories in this city are fast food, continental, Italian and Chinese

4. The subway restaurant has the highest rating from customers because of their special recipes for Italian. fast food, continental , and dessert food category. The female and other gender customers under the teenage age preferred their pasta, pizza, fries and sandwich, and the restaurant generated impressive revenue from cities like Multan city, Lahore city, Pershawar city and Islamabad city.
